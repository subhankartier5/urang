Q & A
When do you deliver in my area?

Depending on where you live we service you on Monday through Saturday.

Q & A
What if nobody is home?

No problem? If you live in a full service building we will take care of everything with your doorman or concierges, so your clothes are waiting for you when you get home. If you do not live in a full service building and you require special delivery instructions, we will do everything possible to accommodate your needs please let us know when filling out the pick-up form, or call us at 800-959-5785. The other way is to designate an appropriate pick-up drop off location and we will take care of the rest.

Q & A
Where do I put my dirty clothes?

Once you are registered, we provide you with a custom u-rang.com garment bag. We will provide you with a personal laminated Identification card is attached to your bag. The bag is returned to you with each completed order. If this is your first order you may use laundry any bag which we will return to you with your new u-rang bag.

Q & A
What if I am not satisfied with a particular item?

Please return it! We will reprocess the garment at no additional charge.

Q & A
How do I pay for the services?

We accept all Major credit cards, including PAYPAL. We can automatically process your credit card or you can send a check - which ever you prefer. Either way, you receive an itemized invoice. We can also bill you on a monthly basis.
